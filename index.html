Games

Tic-Tac-Toe

A classic strategy game where two players take turns marking Xs and Os on a 3x3 grid, aiming to get three in a row.

Rock-Paper-Scissors

A simple hand game where players simultaneously choose rock, paper, or scissors, with each choice defeating one and losing to another.

Connect 4

A two-player connection game where players drop colored discs into a grid, aiming to connect four discs in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
